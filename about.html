Aspiring Cinematographer

Caroline Lathrop is a cinematographer and camera operator based in Brooklyn, New York. She currently attends Pratt Institute for film production with an expected graduation date of May 2023.

Her work ranges from documentary to narrative fiction, exploring stories of community and human connection. She has won awards for documentary filmmaking, specialty film challenges, and she is the 2019 recipient of the JDogg scholarship. 

While she has extensive experience and understanding of the role of a director, she best thrives behind the camera to collaborate and create the visual product of any creative description. In her time at Pratt, she has become familiar with a wide range of cameras and production gear, able to adapt to any set and troubleshoot any complications.
